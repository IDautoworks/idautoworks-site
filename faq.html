Do you do majority acquisitions, or only 100% buyouts?

Both. Majority acquisitions and full buyouts are on the table. We’ll match the structure to your goals.

Do I have to stay involved after the sale?

No. Some owners want a clean exit. Others want to stay for a transition period or keep equity. We can support either.

How confidential is this?

Very. We do not advertise conversations, and we don’t shop your information. We keep it tight.

What makes you different from big chains?

We keep the trust and culture that made the shop successful. Then we add systems, training, and operating discipline that improves outcomes without turning the place into a cookie-cutter franchise.
